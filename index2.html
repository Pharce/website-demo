Meet Dr. Alex Morgan

Board Certified Interventional Radiologist

Dr. Alex Morgan is a leading interventional radiologist with over 15 years of experience in minimally invasive, image-guided procedures. Specializing in vascular and oncologic interventions, Dr. Morgan combines cutting-edge technology with compassionate care.

After completing medical school at Johns Hopkins University, Dr. Morgan pursued specialized training in interventional radiology at Mayo Clinic. Today, Dr. Morgan is recognized as a pioneer in advanced interventional techniques and serves as the Director of Interventional Radiology at Metropolitan Medical Center.

Dr. Morgan is dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans that minimize recovery time and maximize effectiveness, allowing patients to return to their normal lives as quickly as possible.
